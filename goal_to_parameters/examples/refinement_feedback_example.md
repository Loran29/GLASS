# Sample Refinement Feedback

Use this if you want to test the reject-and-refine loop after generating KPIs.

## Example feedback

The rejected KPI is too broad and does not focus on a clearly measurable bottleneck. Replace it with a KPI tied more directly to the waiting time between named activities in the process. Keep the KPI grounded in activities already mentioned, include an explicit unit of measurement, and make the time horizon concrete, such as weekly or monthly over a three-month simulation period.

## Example review approach

- Accept KPIs that clearly map to the main goal.
- Reject KPIs that invent activities, use vague wording, or do not include a strong SMART breakdown.
- Then paste the feedback above into the app and click Refine KPIs.
